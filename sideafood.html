Smart Plate

In this world about 8.9% of the population ie 690 million people go to bed on an empty stomach each night. This fact is enough to make us realise how much food scarce is present, even after being aware of this fact about 30% food is wasted. To solve this we have made a smart plate which automatically calculates the amount of food you shoukd take after analysing your regular trends and rings an alarm.
